Introduction

Welcome to the Midori & Chiaki Relationship Mentions masterpost!

Sometimes, Midori and Chiaki's interactions are very brief in a story. Other times, one of them mentions the other. And there's even times when neither of them are in the story, but their relationship gets mentioned anyway. Whatever it is, this masterpost acts as a collection of all those one-off mentions, especially ones from stories that aren't in the Story List.

Recommended to be enjoyed along with the Pizza & Potato Fries Story List! Now onto the list!
