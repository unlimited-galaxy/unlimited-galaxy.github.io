Answered Question: Ryuseitai’s SEKAI

June 14, 2025 Answered Questions , MidoChia Rambles
3.1k ~3 min

If ryuseitai had a sekai what do you think it would be like? I’ve been wondering it since the prsk collab and I was thinking maybe it would be a city sekai? Or something like that idrk

Sent and answered in June 2025. tweet link of the answer

this ask summoned back the proseka collab i’m crying /lh

i really like the idea of a city for their sekai!! especially since most tokusatsus happen in cities and towns, and cities tend to be the "melting pot", which suits ryuseitai having diverse personalities 😊💞

but i’m sorry, anon… i have…. no thoughts of my own, i feel like you could share more about it than me 😭💔 i haven’t really dipped into proseka long enough to understand its world lore nor how sekai’s work and how they grow and develop… so i haven’t really thought up how a sekai would be for them.

but i asked if a fellow chiakiP would be fine with me sharing their vision, because i really liked it…. so here’s @/RenaiPro’s thoughts on ryuseitai’s sekai! capitalized chunks are from @/RenaiPro, the non-capitalized chunks are my random chip-ins.

"From what I understand, while the person who created the Sekai has the most influence over it over time others who are part of it can end up influencing. So I love the idea of it originally being Chiaki’s, born out of both his desire to be and love of heroes as well as his loneliness and despair. Chiaki’s influences are very obvious at first. But as everyone else joins and as they slowly become a true unit and true friends you see everyone’s influences on the world.

Like it’s originally mostly Chiaki and tiny bits of Kanata. Kanata’s influences slowly growing over time as Ryuseitai becomes his family. As for the juniors, they originally have no influence but once their hearts truly accept Ryuseitai that’s when they start having influence on the Sekai. I also….really love the idea that Midori has no influence on the Sekai until the events of Repayfes….

A little mat you’d see in a dojo popping up for Tetora. A small pond with colorful fishes for Kanata. Midori having no influence adding to his insecurities in Repayfes because “see?! I don’t even influence in the Sekai like everyone else! That’s proof I’m not the same as everyone else!!!" "

"as if it’s proof he should’ve left long ago like tetora said…💔 and the green ranger in the sekai means nothing to him he thinks anyone can replace and be that in his stead 💔"

"Chiaki telling Midori that this Sekai may have been born from Chiaki alone but it was Ryuseitai that nurtured it. It was all five of them together that has made it what it is now. And that while Midori may not SEE his influence on it, it’s still there all the same

And then…after Repayfes when Midori has finally fully devoted himself as a member of Ryuseitai…them going to the Sekai and Midori seeing an adorable little yuruchara plush waiting there…. The other fun thing is to imagine the Ryuseitai Sekai version of the vocaloid characters too since each Sekai gets their own individualized version!

From what I remember at their cores the Sekai versions of the vocaloids are the same, but their personalities are heavily influenced off the one(s) who created the Sekai. In this case going off the idea that the Sekai was born from Chiaki that would mean the vocaloids would all likely represent some aspect of Chiaki’s personality"

"DIBS ON BOOKWORM LEN (no)"

"Heck yeah little bookworm Len (give him glasses too)"

and that’s about all i have to share…! i think it would definitely be so fun and interesting, ryuseitai’s so full of color and personality that there’d be so much to explore!! if you have any thoughts feel free to share, considering we’re about to get proseka x enstars part 2 (global edition)💞
